ABOUT

Discover the Dragon Ball Universe

Dragon Ball, created by Akira Toriyama, is a legendary series that has captivated audiences worldwide. Explore its rich lore, thrilling battles, and unforgettable characters.

From the first Dragon Ball to the latest Dragon Ball Super, experience the evolution of this iconic franchise and its impact on pop culture.

1984: Manga Debut

The original Dragon Ball manga is published, introducing Goku and his adventures.

1986: Anime Launch

The anime adaptation of Dragon Ball airs, bringing the series to television screens.

1996: Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z begins, featuring epic battles and new characters like Vegeta and Frieza.

2009: Dragon Ball Kai

A remastered version of Dragon Ball Z with updated visuals and audio.

2015: Dragon Ball Super

Dragon Ball Super continues the story with new adventures and characters.
